## ABOUT US

## Mission

To give wings to lakhs of dreams of youths from all over Northeast India by making our platform affordable and easily accessible.

## Vision

To be the top upskilling platform of Northeast India and to boost youth employment through practical skills with placement to top organisations and companies in the world.

## Ian Institute is

Ian Institute is the first-ever professional Skill Development Training Institute based in Kokrajhar, Assam. We provide professional training in Aviation and Hotel Management industry and provide quality employment top organisation and companies in the world.

We are on a mission to uplift both mindset and living standards of youths of our region by sharing platform to acquire effective skills to grab opportunity of 21st century also making it easy accessible and most affordable for our people.

Ian Institute is an representation of an ideal " Be the change " by bringing together best experienced personalities and students to change and empower our local economy and its people.

​

Therefore, we collaborated with best trainers in the industry from Senior Human Resource of airline industry to forming alliance with best companies in India both Aviation and star Hotels.
