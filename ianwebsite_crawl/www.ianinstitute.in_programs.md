## Why choose this career?

![salary.jpg](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/e2e661_c719ae949aec475da95417f428bd45b1~mv2.jpg/v1/fill/w_172,h_172,al_c,q_80,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,enc_avif,quality_auto/salary.jpg)

## Attractive salary package

Aviation, Hospitality, and Tourism industries are one of the best industries to start your career because it offers good salary package. Apart from a good salary, it also provides added perks such as free travel and discounts on tickets, accommodations and more.

![salary.jpg](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/5bfb6f_0d9c0ddff0e54fb99dbea4f37fe36111~mv2_d_8576_5696_s_4_2.jpg/v1/fill/w_172,h_172,al_c,q_80,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,enc_avif,quality_auto/5bfb6f_0d9c0ddff0e54fb99dbea4f37fe36111~mv2_d_8576_5696_s_4_2.jpg)

## Opportunities to visit different cities and countries

This course will not just train you for the industry but also allow you to explore different cities and countries. While you are on duty, you will get paid for food, accommodation and transportation by the companies you are working for.

![Traveling Girl](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/426657c5f7984d049d5b33ca667dea4c.jpg/v1/fill/w_172,h_172,al_c,q_80,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,enc_avif,quality_auto/Traveling%20Girl.jpg)

## Flexible work schedules

The advantage of working in Aviation, Hospitality and Tourism industry is that the working hours are flexible. The crews of all aircrafts are required to work for minimum and maximum hours every month.

![Insurance Consultation](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/11062b_bcfb88c448bb40f4a1d81bc7401b878f~mv2.jpg/v1/crop/x_808,y_0,w_3184,h_3200/fill/w_204,h_207,al_c,q_80,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,enc_avif,quality_auto/Insurance%20Consultation.jpg)

## Excessive compensation

Apart from travel perks and an attractive salary, it will provide you with great health and retirement benefits. Moreover, you will also get some additional perks like a discount on flight tickets, hotel bookings and more.

## Luxury lifestyle

You will be benefited with numerous perks that intern help you lead a luxurious life. Whether you are going on a holiday trip with your family or friends, you can make the most of your trip while spending less. Enjoy great discounts on top hotels, exotic food, shopping and transportation.

## Free annual travel with family

Some airlines provide free annual travel expenses for their employees to travel with family. Annual free travel includes free flight tickets for their family members to any destination. Free flight ticket means one can easily save half of the travelling expenses.
