## Mijing Youth Foundation ( Empowering Young Minds )  " Turn Hopes Into Careers — Because Money Shouldn’t Decide Your Destiny "

## About Mijing Youth Foundation NGO

At IIAHM Kokrajhar, we believe that talent, ambition, and hard work should never be held back by financial struggles. Every young person, no matter where they come from or what their family background is, deserves the chance to dream big and build a successful future.

With this mission in mind, our NGO initiative was launched to ensure that no youth’s potential goes to waste simply because of money. Through heavily subsidized hospitality, hotel management, and aviation programs — combined with guaranteed job placement support — we are opening doors for students who would otherwise be left behind.

This initiative carries the message:

“Your Dreams Should Fly — Not Be Limited by Money.”

Because we believe education is not a privilege, it’s a right. Opportunity should belong to everyone — and a brighter future should be within reach for every hardworking, deserving youth.

No financial hurdle should stop a talented student from achieving what they are meant to become. With us, dreams aren’t just encouraged — they are made to fly
